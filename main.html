Featured practice tests

TOEFL

The TOEFL test is the most widely respected English-language test in the world, recognized by more than 10,000 colleges, universities and agencies in more than 130 countries, including Australia, Canada, the U.K. and the United States.

IELTS

IELTS is the high stakes English test for international study, migration and work. Open a world of opportunity with IELTS.

PTE Academic

The Pearson Test of English Academic (PTE Academic) is an English language test designed to assess the readiness of non-native English speakers to participate in a university-level English language instruction program.

OET

OET is a well-respected international English language test for the healthcare sector. Established in the late 1980s under contract to the Australian Federal Government, OET continues to remain relevant through continuous research and validation.

TOEIC

TOEIC is an acronym that stands for Test of English for International Communication. The test evaluates your ability to understand and use English in a professional context.

CAEL

The Canadian Academic English Language (CAEL) Assessment is Canada's leading academic English language test. CAEL is recognized by many international organizations, educational institutions, and government ministries.

CELBAN

The Canadian English Language Benchmark Assessment for Nurses (CELBAN) is a language assessment tool designed specifically for internationally educated nurses who are interested in working in Canada.

MELAB

The Michigan English Language Assessment Battery (MELAB) is a standardized examination developed by the University of Michigan English Language Institute (UMELI). MELAB is designed to measure the English language proficiency of non-native speakers of English.

TEF

The Test d'Évaluation de Français (TEF) is a French language proficiency test for non-native speakers. The exam evaluates your abilities in reading, writing, listening, and speaking.

CPE

The Certificate of Proficiency in English (CPE) is a high-level qualification offered by the University of Cambridge ESOL Examinations. This exam is designed to test your mastery of English and is useful for both academic and professional purposes.
